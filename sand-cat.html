Sand Cat

The Sand Cat (Felis margarita) is a small, elusive wild cat that inhabits the deserts of North Africa, the Middle East, and Central Asia. With its large ears, fluffy paws, and round face, it is perfectly adapted to survive in harsh desert environments, using its thickly furred feet to walk on hot sand without leaving tracks. Despite its adorable appearance, the Sand Cat is a skilled hunter, capable of surviving long periods without water and preying on small rodents, reptiles, and insects.
